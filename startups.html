Opportunities for Entrepreneurs

The VCIC Competition provides a great opportunity for entrepreneurs to practice their pitching skills, have their business evaluated by talented students, and receive feedback from seasoned venture capitalists. Up to 4 companies are recruited to pitch at the event each year. Note that companies involved in the Competition must be currently seeking funding at the time of the Competition in January and must be unknown to the competitors.
